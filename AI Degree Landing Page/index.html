About the Programme

Our BSc in Artificial Intelligence is designed for students who want to lead the next wave of technological change — building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems.

🧠

Machine Learning

Master supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning techniques used by top tech companies worldwide.

📊

Data Science

Learn to collect, clean, analyze, and visualize large datasets to extract meaningful insights and patterns.

🤖

Neural Networks

Understand and build deep learning architectures including CNNs, RNNs, and Transformer models.

💬

Natural Language Processing

Build systems that understand and generate human language — from chatbots to advanced text analysis.

👁️

Computer Vision

Develop AI that can interpret and analyze images and video — powering the next generation of visual applications.

🔬

Research & Ethics

Conduct original research and explore the ethical implications of AI in society, policy, and industry.

Programme Curriculum

A structured four-year journey from foundational computing to cutting-edge AI specializations.

Year 1

Foundations

  • Introduction to Programming (Python)
  • Calculus & Linear Algebra
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Introduction to AI
  • Statistics & Probability
Year 2

Core AI Concepts

  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Machine Learning Fundamentals
  • Database Systems
  • Probability for AI
  • Software Engineering Principles
Year 3

Advanced AI

  • Deep Learning & Neural Networks
  • Computer Vision
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Cloud & MLOps
Year 4

Specialization

  • AI Ethics & Policy
  • Elective Specialization
  • Industry Internship
  • Final Year Research Project
  • Thesis Defense
